Lasagna

A delicious traditional lasagna made with layers of fresh pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy béchamel.

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles;
  • 1 lb ground beef;
  • 24 oz marinara sauce;
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese;
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese;
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese;
  • 1 egg;
  • Salt, black pepper and fresh parsley.

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven: Set your oven to 375 °F (190 °C).
  2. Cook the meat: In a large pan, brown the ground beef until fully cooked. Drain the excess fat and stir in the marinara sauce.
  3. Mix the cheese: In a small bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  4. Layer the lasagna:
    1. Spread a bit of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
    2. Place a layer of noodles on top.
    3. Spread the ricotta mixture, then add a layer of mozzarella.
    4. Repeat the layers until you finish with a thick layer of mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  5. Bake: Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 15 minutes until the top is golden and bubbly.
  6. Rest: Let it sit for 10 minutes before slicing. This helps the layers stay together.
