Pizza

A classic Pizza featuring a thin, crispy crust topped with authentic tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, and fragrant basil leaves.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ball of pizza dough;
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce or crushed San Marzano tomatoes;
  • 4 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced or torn;
  • Fresh basil leaves;
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil;
  • A pinch of salt;
  • All-purpose flour (for dusting);

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven: Place a pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven and preheat it to its highest setting (usually 500 °F (260 °C).
  2. Shape the dough: On a lightly floured surface, stretch the dough into a 12-inch circle.
  3. Add toppings: Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving room for the crust. Add the fresh mozzarella pieces and a pinch of salt.
  4. Bake: Carefully transfer the pizza to the oven. Bake for 8~12 minutes until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and slightly charred.
  5. Finish: Remove from the oven and immediately top with fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil.
  6. Serve: Let it cool for 2 minutes before slicing.
